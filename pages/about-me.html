About Me

Hi! My name is Jeffrey Luppes. I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist based in Amsterdam.

Apart from five internships as part of my degrees I've had the pleasure of working with many talented people at TNO, Atos and DPG Media. For TNO, I worked on ETL processes allowing the projects of data scientists and web-scraping. At Atos I worked for clients all over Europe, working mainly on cloud projects and projects based around text categorization and NER. I'm currently a ML Engineer for DPG, where I'm trying to improve the world of recruitment by creating and deploying models to extract information from job postings, amongst other things. Throughout the years I've worked with GCP and AWS.

I blog on Towardsdatascience about things in the ML Engineering space.

Like many engineers I like to work on my own projects from time to time - be that web scraping and using the data in some cool way, or creating a bird recognition model for my balcony.

Outside of data science I enjoy camping, birdwatching, bicycling, and sea kayaking. Jeffreykajakt.com is my (Dutch) kayaking site but occasionally I post there in English. I'd like to one day kayak around Iceland - other than that, no big plans.
